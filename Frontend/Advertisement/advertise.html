Advertisement Policy

At Health Sewa, we believe in transparency when it comes to advertising on our platform. This Advertisement Policy outlines how we manage, display, and approve ads.

1. Ad Relevance

We only display ads that are relevant to our users and align with our health-first mission. Advertisements promoting harmful, misleading, or unrelated content will not be permitted.

2. Third-Party Advertisements

Health Sewa may feature third-party ads that are clearly marked as "Sponsored" or "Ad". These third-party advertisers may collect data as per their own privacy policies.

3. No Endorsement

Displayed ads do not mean we endorse the products or services advertised. We advise users to exercise discretion before making decisions based on ads.

4. Ethical Guidelines

Ads that are discriminatory, explicit, exploitative, or violate any law will not be approved or shown on our platform.

5. User Control

Users may opt-out of personalized ads through their browser settings. However, non-personalized ads may still appear.

6. Reporting Concerns

If you see an ad that violates this policy or seems suspicious, please report it to us at: ads@healthsewa.in
