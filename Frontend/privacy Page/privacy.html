Privacy Policy

At Health Sewa, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Register as a user or recipient
  • Submit health-related queries or information
  • Use features like disease info, blood bank locator, or contact forms

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized health content and assistance
  • Show blood donation requests and recipient details
  • Respond to your messages and support requests

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is never shared without your consent, except when required by law.

4. Cookies

Health Sewa uses cookies to enhance user experience. These cookies do not collect personal data and are used for analytics only.

5. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy and terms.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you about changes by posting the updated policy here.
