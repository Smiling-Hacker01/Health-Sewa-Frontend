High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)

Know the signs. Understand the causes. Take control of your health.

⚠️ High blood pressure is a silent killer — often showing no symptoms until it's dangerous.

🩺 Common Symptoms

  • Severe headaches
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nosebleeds (in some cases)
  • Blurred or double vision
  • Chest pain
  • Fatigue or confusion
  • Irregular heartbeat

🔍 Common Causes

  • Unhealthy diet (high in salt, sugar, saturated fats)
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Chronic stress or anxiety
  • Obesity or being overweight
  • Genetics and family history
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Age (risk increases as you grow older)

🛡️ Prevention Tips

✅ Daily Habits to Adopt:

  • Walk at least 30 minutes daily 🏃‍♂️
  • Practice deep breathing or meditation for 10 minutes 🧘‍♀️
  • Drink 2–3 liters of water daily 💧
  • Get 7–8 hours of quality sleep 😴
  • Monitor your BP regularly 📊
  • Limit screen time before bed 📱

🚫 Foods to Avoid:

  • Processed and packaged foods (high sodium content)
  • Fried and junk foods
  • Excessive caffeine and alcohol
  • Red meat in excess
  • Sugary drinks and desserts

Bonus Tip: Include potassium-rich foods like bananas, spinach, sweet potatoes, and avocados in your diet.

🧠 Why You Should Care

Uncontrolled high blood pressure increases the risk of heart attacks, strokes, kidney failure, and vision loss. But the good news? It's manageable through lifestyle changes and, if needed, medication.
