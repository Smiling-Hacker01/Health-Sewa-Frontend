Disease Information

Fever

Causes: Infections, heat exhaustion.

Symptoms: High temperature, chills, sweating.

Treatment: Rest, hydration, fever-reducing medication.

Common Cold

Causes: Rhinovirus infection.

Symptoms: Sneezing, runny nose, sore throat.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, vitamin C, steam inhalation.

Flu (Influenza)

Causes: Influenza virus.

Symptoms: Fever, chills, fatigue, cough.

Treatment: Antiviral meds, rest, hydration.

COVID-19

Causes: SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Symptoms: Cough, fever, difficulty breathing.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, medical supervision if severe.

Diabetes

Causes: Insulin resistance, genetics.

Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue.

Treatment: Diet control, insulin therapy, exercise.

Hypertension

Causes: Stress, obesity, high salt intake.

Symptoms: Dizziness, headaches, blurred vision.

Treatment: Lifestyle changes, medication.

Asthma

Causes: Allergens, smoke, genetic factors.

Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, coughing.

Treatment: Inhalers, avoiding triggers.

Migraine

Causes: Stress, bright lights, dehydration.

Symptoms: Severe headache, nausea, sensitivity to light.

Treatment: Painkillers, resting in a dark room.

Pneumonia

Causes: Bacteria, viruses, fungi.

Symptoms: Cough with phlegm, fever, chest pain.

Treatment: Antibiotics, hydration, oxygen therapy.

Typhoid

Causes: Salmonella bacteria (contaminated food/water).

Symptoms: Fever, weakness, loss of appetite.

Treatment: Antibiotics, hydration.

Dengue Fever

Causes: Mosquito-borne virus.

Symptoms: High fever, joint pain, rash.

Treatment: Hydration, pain relievers.

Malaria

Causes: Mosquito-borne parasite.

Symptoms: Fever, chills, nausea.

Treatment: Antimalarial drugs.

Headache

Causes: Stress, dehydration, lack of sleep.

Symptoms: Pain in the head/forehead, pressure.

Treatment: Hydration, rest, painkillers.

Causes: Stress, dehydration, lack of sleep.

Symptoms: Pain in the body, pressure, joint pain, headache.

Treatment: Hydration, rest, painkillers.
